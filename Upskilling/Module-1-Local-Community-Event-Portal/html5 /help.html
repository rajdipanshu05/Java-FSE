Help & FAQs

Welcome to the Community Portal Help Centre. Find answers to common questions below.

How do I register for an event?

Go to the Register section on the home page, fill in your details, select an event, and click "Register Now".

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes. Contact the City Council office at least 48 hours before the event date.

How do I find events near me?

Use the "Find Nearby Events" button in the Location section. Allow browser location access when prompted.
