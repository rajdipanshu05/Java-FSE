Community Bulletin

The city council has approved a new community park in the east district. Construction begins next month and is expected to complete by September 2025. Residents are invited to attend public consultations every Tuesday evening. A brand-new cycling track will also be included in the park layout. Volunteers for the green-drive initiative can sign up at the council office. The annual flower festival has been moved to the central plaza this year. Free yoga classes will be offered every Saturday morning in the park. All feedback can be submitted through this portal or at the town hall.
